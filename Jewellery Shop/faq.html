Frequently Asked Questions

1. How long does delivery take?

Delivery usually takes 3-7 business days depending on your location.

2. What is your return policy?

You can return jewellery within 15 days of purchase. Items must be unused and in original packaging.

3. Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we ship worldwide. Shipping charges vary depending on the destination.

4. Can I customize jewellery?

Yes! Please contact us via the Contact page for custom orders.

5. How can I track my order?

Once your order is shipped, we will provide a tracking number via email.
