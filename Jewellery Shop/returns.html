Returns & Exchanges

1. Return Period: You can return your jewellery within 15 days of delivery. All items must be unused and in their original packaging.

2. Exchange: You can exchange any product for a different size or style within the same 15-day period. Please contact us for assistance.

3. Refund Process: Once we receive the returned item and verify its condition, the refund will be processed within 5-7 business days to your original payment method.

4. Shipping Charges: Customers are responsible for the shipping cost for returns unless the product is defective.

5. Contact for Returns: Please reach out via our Contact Page before sending any returns or exchanges.
