Shipping Information

1. Delivery Time: Orders are typically delivered within 3-7 business days. Delivery time may vary depending on your location.

2. Shipping Charges: Standard shipping is free on orders above ₹2000. For orders below ₹2000, shipping charges apply as per your location.

3. International Shipping: We offer worldwide shipping. Shipping charges and delivery time vary by country.

4. Tracking Your Order: Once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email to monitor the delivery status.

5. Shipping Issues: For any delay or issue with delivery, please contact us via our Contact Page.
